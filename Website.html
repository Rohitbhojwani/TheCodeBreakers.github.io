About Us

The CodeBreakers Club is a club of Shri Ramdeobaba College Of Engineering And Management, Nagpur, Maharashtra-440013. The CodeBreakers Club comes under the Technical Club and has a speciality of Programming languages, Competitive coding, Web Developemnt, IOT, AIML, Open Source Intelligence and many more. The club is a SRC (Student's Representative Counsil) which is a student body ran by the cuurent students studying in the college. The club organises many events for the college and represents RCOEM in different events held over All over India.

Events

Club conducts Competitive Coding Challenge Events, one of the many competitions held under Branch Cup, which is organized by Students’ Representative Council.

TechnoVision

Technovision 6.0 was a land mark event as it was inaugurated and graced by the presence of Honorable former president of India and well known Scientist Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam in 2010. Now this event has become the brand event of RCOEM.TechnoVision is a unique and proven source of change-making advice that is written by our leading experts. TechnoVision 2021 builds on our long-standing heritage of expert guidance – it’s the 13th edition of our annual technology guide.

Ethical Hacking Fest

Ethical Hacking Fest is conducted by our club where people from all over the country come and participate in various competitions like Bug Bounty hunting, Penetration testing, Wifi hacking and many more.There are rewards for every competition and it is conducted every year in RCOEM.
